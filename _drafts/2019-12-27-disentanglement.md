We must be careful to separate terminology. In one case we have diffusions *on* manifolds
in which we have random walks on manifolds. In the other case we have diffusions defined
on *metrics* of manifolds. As the metric itself defines the geometry of the manifold, the 
whole object changes structure through the diffusion. These are also known as geometric flows.
